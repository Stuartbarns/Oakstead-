Our Story

Built by the communities it serves.

Oakstead Light Foundation started with a simple observation: young people and families across West Nile's rural districts had little safe, judgment-free access to sexual and reproductive health information, or to mental health support after crisis. We set out to close that gap — not with clinics from the outside, but with trained local volunteers who already have the community's trust.

Today, our teams run outreach sessions, peer counseling, and school-based education programs across Zombo, Pakwachi, and Nebbi — funded almost entirely by individual donors and supported by a growing volunteer network.
